These can start as agent-suggested working hypotheses during onboarding and get refined later.

Track priority topics here.

- keyword: open source seo tools
  - intent: Commercial investigation
  - priority: High
  - notes: Core category term that matches product positioning. Good pillar topic and comparison hub.

- keyword: semrush alternative
  - intent: Commercial investigation
  - priority: High
  - notes: Strong fit for switchers evaluating established platforms. Needs careful messaging around fit, tradeoffs, and evidence.

- keyword: ahrefs alternative
  - intent: Commercial investigation
  - priority: High
  - notes: Similar to Semrush-alternative intent. Best approached with transparent comparison content rather than parity claims.

- keyword: self hosted seo tools
  - intent: Commercial investigation
  - priority: High
  - notes: Strong differentiator term aligned with OpenSEO's control and flexibility angle.

- keyword: technical seo tools
  - intent: Commercial investigation
  - priority: High
  - notes: Broad but relevant category for the most natural primary audience.
